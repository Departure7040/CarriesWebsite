# Review Request Templates

**Purpose:** Ready-to-send templates for building sustained review volume (target: 2–4 new reviews/month, not one-time bursts — see `/reports/02_local_seo_audit.md` Section (d)). All templates are policy-compliant: no incentives, no gating, every client gets the same public link regardless of anticipated sentiment. `[best-practice]`

**Before sending:** Replace `[LINK]` placeholders with the actual live review link once available:
- Google review link: `[client-confirm — available once GBP is created, see 02_local_seo_audit.md Section a-1]`
- Zillow review link: `https://www.zillow.com/profile/carriebilleaud#reviews` (Carrie's strongest existing platform — 29 reviews `[verified]`)

Tone: friendly, warm, Louisiana-casual without being unprofessional. Keep texts under 3-4 sentences; emails slightly longer but still skimmable.

## 6. Response Templates

### Responding to a Positive Review

> Thank you so much, [Reviewer First Name]! It was such a pleasure helping you [find your home in Youngsville / sell your home / navigate your first purchase] — wishing you all the best in your new place. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you ever need anything! — Carrie

`[best-practice]` Customize the bracketed detail to something true and specific from the actual transaction when known; avoid a fully generic response. Respond within 48 hours.

### Responding to a Negative Review

> Thank you for sharing this, [Reviewer First Name]. I take feedback like this seriously and I'm sorry to hear your experience didn't meet expectations. I'd really like to understand what happened and see if there's anything I can do to make it right — please give me a call at [canonical phone — pending client confirmation, see 02_local_seo_audit.md] or email me directly at [canonical email — pending client confirmation]. — Carrie

`[best-practice]` Keep the public response short, professional, and non-defensive. Never argue details publicly or imply the reviewer is wrong. Move the conversation offline. Respond within 24–48 hours. Only pursue formal removal through the platform's policy process if the review is clearly illegitimate (not an actual client, fake account, hate speech) — do not pressure a genuine client to delete a genuine complaint.
