### 1 · Website — demo: `/` (the live homepage)
Lead with the homepage — the magic moment is her SEEING her own current listings
load without anyone touching them; say "this updates itself, you'll never re-type
a listing." Then one area page (`/areas/lafayette.html`) so she gets the local-SEO
point: "this is how buyers searching Youngsville or Broussard find YOU." Close on
`/testimonials.html` — point at the gold stars and say "Google can show these,
that's trust before they even click." Keep it to those three clicks; don't tour all

### 2 · Drive leads — demo: `/l/101-rio-ridge-dr/?utm_source=instagram`

that lands the "the page KNOWS" moment. Then flip to `/studio/attribution.html`

### 3 · AI — demo: `/studio/nurture.html`
Hook first: "How many leads have gone cold because the fourth follow-up never got
sent?" Open the nurture desk, let her SEE a full sequence already drafted, tap

check. Then set up go-live: "the other half is the 24/7 chat responder — a lead

that switches on the day we launch with your key." **Do NOT click anything
chat-related — it's not live yet.**

### 4 · Social media — demo: `/studio/#listing-videos`

just post" — zero design work, zero caption-writing. Mention lightly that at go-live

already done for her current listings. Nothing auto-posts.

### 5 · Content creation — demo: `/studio/`
