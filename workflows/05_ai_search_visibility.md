# Workflow 05 — AI Search Visibility

**Delegate to:** `agents/ai_search_visibility_agent.md`

## Framing (non-negotiable)
AI search visibility = **entity clarity + authority + useful crawlable content**.
Google AI Overviews / AI Mode ride on core Search quality systems. ChatGPT-style
tools lean on consistent public entity data, authoritative third-party mentions,
and crawlable pages. There is no separate magic trick.

## Steps
1. From WF01 data, assess entity consistency: does every public surface agree on
   who Carrie is, where she serves, what she specializes in, how to contact her?
2. Score current state: bio quality, NAP consistency, structured data presence,
   review depth, third-party authority (Zillow/Realtor/Homes/news/local orgs).
3. Recommend fixes so AI systems can answer:
   - Who is Carrie Billeaud? (entity bio, sameAs links, schema)
   - Where does she serve? (explicit service-area statements)
   - What does she specialize in? (first-time buyers, sellers, residential)
   - Why is she credible? (verified metrics only, reviews, associations)
   - How to contact her? (one canonical phone/address everywhere)
4. Recommend content structures AI systems cite: clear Q&A/FAQ content, locally
   specific market pages, plain-language bios.

## Prohibited recommendations
Fake mentions, fabricated citations, llms.txt-as-ranking-tactic, AI-spam pages,
review gating/incentivized reviews.
