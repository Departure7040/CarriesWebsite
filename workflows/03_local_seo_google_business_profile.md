# Workflow 03 — Local SEO / Google Business Profile Audit

**Delegate to:** `agents/local_seo_analyst.md`

## Premise
Local ranking = relevance + distance + prominence (Google's public guidance).
We may not have GBP access; if the profile URL is unknown, produce a
"client must provide" section rather than guessing.

## Steps
1. Determine GBP status from WF01 findings (found / not found / unclaimed?).
2. Build full GBP optimization checklist: business name policy compliance,
   primary + secondary categories, services, service areas, description,
   attributes, hours, phone (must match canonical NAP), website link with UTM,
   booking/appointment links.
3. Review strategy: acquisition cadence, request timing, channels (text/email),
   response guidance (all reviews, incl. negative), keyword-natural responses.
4. Photo strategy: types, cadence, geotagging myths to avoid.
5. Q&A strategy: seed legitimate FAQs, monitor.
6. Posting strategy: cadence, post types (listings, market updates, closings
   w/ permission, local events), UTM tagging.
7. Service-area strategy for Lafayette, Youngsville, Broussard, Carencro,
   Scott, Maurice, Milton.
