The real cash to close — the part that surprises people

Earnest money (~1% of price) is paid when your offer is accepted and credited back to you at closing — not an extra cost, but cash you need available early. Inspection & appraisal are paid out of pocket during the process, before closing.

Assumptions used (all estimates, adjustable in a lender conversation): back-end DTI 43% / front-end 30%; property tax ~0.5% of value (Louisiana is low; homestead lowers it); homeowners insurance ~1% (Acadiana runs high); flood ~$900/yr when flagged; PMI ~0.8%/yr if under 20% down; closing costs ~3% (range 2–5%); prepaids ~1.25%; reserves 2 months of payment.
