The Home

Considered Comfort at 110 Marais Avenue

Set behind a manicured lawn on a fenced 0.2-acre lot, this 2018-built brick residence reads as unhurried and sure of itself. A peaked roofline and attached two-car garage anchor the façade, while neighboring homes frame a settled, established street.

Inside, 2,625 square feet unfold in an open-concept plan where a dark exposed beam crosses the living room and a teal accent wall meets crisp white trim. The kitchen and dining area flow toward glass doors that open onto a fenced backyard patio — the natural center of the home.

With five bedrooms and two and a half baths across two levels, the layout leaves room to spread out: a lower-level family room, a dedicated laundry room, and gathering spaces that shift easily between quiet evenings and full houses.

Restraint, not spectacle — a home built in 2018 and finished with an even hand.

The Setting

110 Marais Avenue sits in Youngsville, Louisiana, in the heart of the Acadiana region of the state's south. The setting is residential and established, with neighboring homes lining a tidy, fenced street.

The 0.2-acre parcel offers a manicured front lawn and a fully fenced backyard, giving the property a defined sense of place within its community.
