The Residence

A Study in Composure

Set on just over half an acre in south Lafayette, 101 Rio Ridge Drive is a new-construction estate that trades spectacle for composure. Completed in 2024, its 6,800 square feet unfold without haste — rooms sized for gathering, thresholds that open to the pool and the covered loggia, light that moves through the day and settles by evening.

Five bedrooms and five-and-a-half-plus baths are arranged for both retreat and welcome. The living spaces reach outward to a private pool and spillover spa, an outdoor kitchen, and a covered living area warmed by a fireplace — where the boundary between house and landscape is drawn lightly and crossed often. A four-car garage anchors the practical side of a house built for a life lived fully.

This is a single property, offered once. What follows is not a list of amenities but an account of a place — private, newly made, and finished to the standard its address suggests.

Some houses announce themselves. This one simply waits, and lets you arrive.

Location

The Setting

Set in south Lafayette's 70508 area, 101 Rio Ridge Drive sits in a residential setting that is also central to the city. Everyday conveniences are close at hand, while the surrounding countryside lies just beyond. The immediate streets are quiet and residential.

The location places daily life within a short drive of nearly everything Lafayette offers. Ambassador Caffery Parkway and Verot School Road anchor the area's shopping, dining, and services, while River Ranch and the greater Camellia Boulevard district provide a walkable mix of boutiques, cafes, and restaurants a few minutes away. Downtown Lafayette, the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, and the region's medical centers are all comfortably reachable, and connections to US-90 and the wider Acadiana road network make travel across the parish and beyond straightforward.

Beyond convenience, the address carries the distinctive character of Cajun and Creole Louisiana, a region known for its cuisine, live music, and celebrated festival calendar. Lakes, parks, and bayou-country recreation lie within easy reach, offering a genuine sense of place that pairs well with the refinement of the home itself.
