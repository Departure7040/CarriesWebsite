About Carrie

A Lafayette Realtor with Acadiana roots

Carrie Billeaud is a REALTOR® with eXp Realty, serving buyers and sellers throughout Lafayette and the surrounding Acadiana communities of Youngsville, Broussard, Carencro, Scott, Maurice and Milton. She holds a business degree from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, which shapes an analytical, detail-driven approach to pricing, negotiation and market timing. Carrie works alongside her father as part of her team, and brings a background in home staging to the table — helping sellers present their homes at their best before they hit the market. Her focus areas are first-time home buyers navigating their first purchase and sellers who want a straightforward, well-managed listing process, along with residential and investment property transactions for clients across the Lafayette area. Whether you're weighing your first offer or getting a home ready to list, Carrie walks through each step so you know what to expect next. Clients rate her work 5.0 stars across 185 Google reviews and 29 more on Zillow.

FAQ

Common questions

Does it cost anything to work with a buyer's agent?

Buyer-agent compensation depends on the listing, the offer terms, and your buyer-agency agreement. Carrie will walk through the options upfront so you understand what, if anything, you may be responsible for before you tour homes.

How fast do homes sell in Lafayette?

Time on market varies by neighborhood, price point and condition, so there isn't one universal number for all of Lafayette. Carrie can pull current comparable listings for your specific area and price range to give you a realistic estimate.

What are the first steps for a first-time buyer?

Start with a mortgage pre-approval so you know your price range, then talk through what you need versus want in a home. From there, Carrie can set up a search tailored to your budget and priorities across Lafayette and the surrounding areas.

What's my home worth?

Home values depend on recent comparable sales, condition and current market activity in your specific neighborhood. Request a free valuation below and Carrie will put together a comparison based on your address.
