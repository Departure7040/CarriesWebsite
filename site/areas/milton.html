Comparing the Corridor

Youngsville, Maurice, or Milton? Comparing the Three

These three communities sit close together south of Lafayette, but they serve different buyers. Here's an honest look at how they compare — not a push toward any one of them.

Youngsville

New construction, master-planned communities like Sugar Mill Pond, and Acadiana's fastest growth. Best fit for buyers who want amenities and a subdivision lifestyle. See the Youngsville page →

Maurice

Land, acreage and rural privacy just south of Lafayette. Best fit for buyers who want space, outbuildings or room for animals rather than a subdivision lot. See the Maurice page →

Milton

A small, quiet, family-oriented community close to Youngsville's conveniences without Youngsville's density or price premium. Best fit for buyers who want a tight-knit small-town feel first.
