Your Content Studio

Every new listing on your feed is turned into a ready-to-post package — branded graphic + a caption written for each platform. Review, approve, copy, post.

How this works. When a new listing hits your feed, the engine drafts the captions and renders your branded graphics automatically. Here you just review, tweak in your head, and one-tap copy the caption, then download the graphic and post it yourself on each platform. Publishing stays in your hands for now (or later, hand the approved batch to a scheduling aggregator like Buffer/Metricool) — nothing is ever auto-posted. You are always the voice and the compliance check.

Each caption now ends with a tracked link to that home's landing page — a different link per platform, so every click and lead ties back to the exact post it came from. See the Attribution dashboard →.
