Follow-Up Drafts

Every lead who isn't ready today gets a warm, low-pressure follow-up sequence — drafted for you, step by step. You review each message, then approve, edit, or skip. Nothing sends on its own.

These are drafts — Carrie approves before anything sends. The engine drafts each step of a lead's follow-up sequence (a warm welcome, a criteria-matched touch, a useful resource, a gentle check-in). You Approve, Edit, or Skip each one. Approval is the only thing that would ever make a message sendable — and even then, sending happens through the messaging gateway wired at deploy, never from this page.

Every message carries its opt-out line (SMS: Reply STOP to opt out; email: an unsubscribe line) — TCPA for texts, CAN-SPAM for email. Content is held to the same fair-housing / facts-only rules as the rest of the site. Consent is required before a real lead is ever enqueued. Approvals here are saved in your browser only (demo).
