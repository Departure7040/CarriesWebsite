Skip These

5 things NOT to waste money on

  • Backlink packages / "citation blasts" — spam that risks more entity confusion, the exact problem we're fixing.
  • "AI SEO" gimmicks (llms.txt hacks, AI-written page farms) — AI visibility comes from the same fundamentals as Google visibility.
  • A paid website rebuild before the basics — the eXp site has limits, but bio, metadata and content fixes come first and are free.
  • Buying leads from portals as a substitute for this work — rented visibility disappears the day you stop paying.
  • Review-gating or incentivized-review tools — against Google policy and can get reviews removed or the profile suspended.
