Reviews

Review strategy: maintain the lead, close the other gaps

With Google already at 185 reviews / 5.0, the priority shifts from "catch up" to "don't let it stall" — while building up Realtor.com (2 reviews) and Homes.com (0 reviews), which are the real gaps now. Verified

  • Pace: 2–4 new reviews per month, sustained indefinitely, split across Google (keep extending the lead) and the lagging platforms. Bursts of reviews look unnatural to spam detection and create a visible cliff in the timeline. Best practice
  • Timing: Ask within 24–48 hours of closing, while the experience is freshest. A secondary ask at the 6-month mark recaptures clients who didn't respond the first time.
  • Channels: Text for the highest response rate, email with a personal opening line, and a verbal ask at the closing table to prime the follow-up.
  • Policy: No incentives, no review-gating (privately screening for happy clients before directing them to the public link), no posting on a client's behalf. Every template used directs every client to the same public link regardless of anticipated sentiment. Best practice
  • Responses: Reply to positive reviews within 48 hours, specific and brief. Reply to negative reviews within 24–48 hours, professionally, offer to resolve offline, and never argue publicly.
