Free · Monthly Market Report

Get the Monthly Acadiana Market Report

Each month, Carrie compiles the Acadiana numbers — median sale price, days on market, inventory, months of supply, and a by-town breakdown — into one clean, sourced report. Enter your name and email and it lands in your inbox.

Get the report ↓ See a sample (PDF) →

What's In It

The Acadiana numbers, in one place

Median sale price

The midpoint sale price for the area and for each town, so you can see where prices sit this month.

Days on market

How long a home that sold spent on the market, on average, over the period.

Inventory & months of supply

How many homes are actively listed, and how that compares to the pace of sales.

Closed sales

How many transactions actually closed in the period — area-wide and by town.

Price per square foot

The median price per square foot, a useful cross-check when comparing homes of different sizes.

By-town breakdown

Lafayette, Youngsville, Broussard, Carencro, Scott, Maurice and Milton — each with the same measures.

Every figure in the report is drawn from a dated, labeled data source. The report describes the numbers only — it is not a forecast, a valuation, or advice on whether to buy or sell.
