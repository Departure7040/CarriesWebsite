Why Sell With a Local Agent

A local full-service agent vs. a discount brokerage or iBuyer

A discount or flat-fee brokerage typically means less marketing, less negotiation support and less hands-on guidance for the same transaction. An iBuyer or cash-offer company buys directly from you, usually below what a marketed, negotiated sale would bring. A full-service local agent like Carrie markets your home to real buyers, negotiates on your behalf, and stays involved through closing — the trade-off is a service fee for that added value. Which route makes sense depends on your priorities: speed and certainty, or maximizing your sale price with full support.
